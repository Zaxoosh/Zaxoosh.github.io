Secret AI Project

During my time working as the Head of Staff for a competitor to OpenAI's Chatgpt, I played a critical role in establishing and growing the project's community. As the equivalent of a Discord Community Manager, my responsibilities involved organizing the organizational structure of the project and filling various positions through a rigorous interview process.

In addition to these responsibilities, I also created an online clock-in system to monitor staff activity and progression. This helped us to ensure that all team members were contributing their fair share and working towards our common goals.

To further engage and excite our community, I organized several contests, including an art contest, which drew in many talented artists who submitted their work for consideration. These contests helped us to build a sense of community and fostered a spirit of friendly competition among our members.

Thanks to these efforts and our team's hard work, we were able to grow our community to an impressive 20,000 members in just three days! We also made continuous updates to the Discord server and eventually moved over to Matrix via Element to better manage and scale our operations.

As part of my efforts to improve our management and moderation, I created and set up a bot system that integrated into the server. This system helped to automate many of the moderation and management tasks, freeing up our team members to focus on more creative and strategic work.

Overall, my work as the Head of Staff for this competitor to OpenAI's Chatgpt helped to establish and grow a vibrant community around our project. I am proud to have played a critical role in this success and to have contributed to the growth and development of the project.
